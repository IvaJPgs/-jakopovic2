WE ARE HAMILTON

We are Hamilton Hospiality

Hamilton Hospitality is a multinational hospitality company that operates a portfolio of hotel brands, including Marriott Hotels Resorts, JW Marriott, The Ritz-Carlton, Renaissance Hotels, Courtyard by Marriott, and many others. With over 7,600 properties across 133 countries and territories, Marriott International is one of the largest hotel companies in the world. The company is known for its commitment to guest satisfaction and providing exceptional customer service, as well as its efforts to promote sustainability and social responsibility. At Hamilton Hospitality, we believe that hospitality is an art, and our team of dedicated professionals is committed to mastering that art with every guest interaction. Whether you're seeking a luxurious stay at one of our upscale hotels, hosting a grand event at our stunning venues, or indulging in culinary delights at our renowned restaurants, we ensure that every detail is meticulously curated to exceed your expectations.

We have the best gardens

Welcome to Hotel Gardens, where luxury meets nature's embrace! Nestled amidst lush greenery and blooming flora, our enchanting oasis offers a tranquil haven for the discerning traveler seeking a respite from the bustling city life. As you step foot into our exquisite property, you'll be captivated by the sight of manicured gardens that stretch as far as the eye can see. Vibrant flowers, cascading waterfalls, and meandering pathways weave together to create a harmonious tapestry of natural beauty. The fragrant aromas of blossoms waft through the air, rejuvenating your senses and instilling a sense of serenity within. Our commitment to creating an immersive botanical experience is reflected in every aspect of our hotel. Each guest room and suite overlooks the verdant gardens, providing a picturesque view that transports you to a world of tranquility. The soothing sounds of chirping birds and rustling leaves form an enchanting symphony, ensuring a peaceful night's sleep and a gentle awakening each morning.
