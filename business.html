The Hamilton Congress Centers

Zagreb Fair’s corporate project ZeVent is based on organizing innovative event concepts as well as on hosting the event industry. For this purpose, Zagreb Fair offers numerous conference halls, exhibition and event spaces able to host a large number of participants. Ten pavilions covering more than 40,000 m2, the open-air space of 30,000 m2, the Congress Center with the seating capacity of up to 1,500 and the parking lot of almost 90,000 m2 are the portfolio for creative projects and successful organization.

The Congress Hall boasts one of the largest seating capacities in the city of Zagreb. Due to its excellent traffic connection with the airport and the city center as well as secured parking space the organization of events is logistically acceptable.

Zagreb ima dugu sveučilišnu tradiciju. Prvu je svjetovnu gradsku školu dobio još sredinom 14. stoljeća, početkom 16. stoljeća utemeljena je prva gimnazija, a u drugoj polovici istoga stoljeća sveučilište. Ono je utemeljeno 1669. godine, jedno je od najstarijih u Europi te će 2019. godine obilježiti 350 godina postojanja.
